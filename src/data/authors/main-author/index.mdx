Christopher A. Rotunno is an analytics engineer based in St. Augustine, FL. He's spent a decade across fintech, retail, and operations — where the same challenge kept surfacing: fragmented data, competing versions of truth, and teams making decisions without enough visibility.

He builds the infrastructure that changes that. But the part he finds most interesting isn't the pipeline — it's what happens when the right person finally has the right information at the right moment. That's what the work is actually for.

His writing covers the human side of data: building systems that earn trust, navigating the gap between what stakeholders ask for and what they actually need, and the lessons that only show up once the dashboard goes live.
