A poorly designed warehouse accumulates technical debt faster than any other part of the stack. I design data models with the right grain, the right structure, and the right tradeoffs documented — so your team can build on top of them for years.

## What's Included

- Dimensional modeling and star schema design
- Warehouse setup and configuration (BigQuery, Redshift)
- Staging layer design and source alignment
- Mart layer modeling for business domains
- Metrics layer design for consistent KPI definitions

## Platforms

BigQuery · Redshift · Python · Spark · Airflow

I design for the questions the business is actually asking — not the schema that was easiest to load.
