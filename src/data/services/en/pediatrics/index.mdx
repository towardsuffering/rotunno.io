Not sure if you can trust your data? A structured audit surfaces the gaps before they become incidents. I review your pipeline, models, and BI layer and deliver a prioritized roadmap for improvement.

## What Gets Reviewed

- Source data quality and freshness SLAs
- dbt model structure, grain clarity, and test coverage
- BI layer consistency (metric definitions, filter logic)
- Documentation completeness
- Incremental model design and warehouse cost efficiency

## Deliverables

Written audit report · Prioritized issue list · Recommended remediation roadmap · Optional follow-on implementation

An audit is the fastest way to understand what you have, what's working, and what's at risk.
