Dashboards that don't get used are expensive mistakes. I design BI solutions around the decision they need to support — the right KPIs, a clean visual hierarchy, and self-serve layouts that reduce ad-hoc requests and increase data literacy across teams.

## What's Included

- KPI definition workshops with stakeholders
- Dashboard wireframing and prototype review
- Production build in Tableau, Plotly, or Google Data Studio
- Row-level security and permission configuration
- Handoff documentation and stakeholder training

## Platforms

Tableau · Plotly · Google Data Studio · Excel · Metabase

I've shipped dashboards for executive teams, sales ops, finance, marketing, and customer success — and I measure success by adoption, not aesthetics.
