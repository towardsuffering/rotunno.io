## Project Overview

A finance and sales leadership team was running monthly business reviews from 40+ separate Excel files built by individual analysts. The process took 3 days to assemble and was frequently inconsistent between regions. I designed and shipped a unified Power BI suite that consolidated all reporting into a single, governed, refreshable source of truth.

## Technical Implementation

Source data from three ERP systems was landed in Snowflake via Fivetran. I built a dimensional model in dbt — dim_customer, dim_product, dim_date, fct_revenue — with full test coverage and documentation. The Power BI dataset connected directly to Snowflake in DirectQuery mode for near-real-time refresh.

Key engineering decisions: incremental materialization on the fact table to keep query cost low at scale; row-level security implemented at the warehouse level and mirrored in Power BI; a centralized metrics layer in dbt for consistent revenue definitions across all reports.

## Outcome

Reporting cycle dropped from 3 days to 4 hours. Dashboard adopted by 200+ users within 60 days of launch. Finance team reduced ad-hoc analyst requests by an estimated 40%.
