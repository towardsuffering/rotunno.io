## Project Overview

AE Global Solutions operated a financial data platform serving clients who needed reliable, accurate market data around the clock. The data infrastructure ran continuously across multiple time zones, with no tolerance for downtime or data quality failures.

## Technical Implementation

Day-to-day work involved maintaining and improving the ETL pipelines that ingested, validated, and distributed financial data from multiple upstream sources. Pipeline efficiency improvements came from profiling slow transformation steps, rewriting bottleneck queries, and introducing parallel processing where the data flow allowed.

Data quality was tracked at every stage: record counts, schema conformance, value range checks, and cross-source reconciliation. Anomalies triggered alerts before downstream consumers were affected.

## Outcome

Maintained 99% uptime across 24/7 operations for the duration of the role. The 30% pipeline efficiency gain reduced processing latency during peak market hours. Partnerships with S&P and Reuters expanded the platform's data product catalog by 120%, opening new revenue streams for the business.
